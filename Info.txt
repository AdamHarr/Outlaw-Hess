This application is for managers of Outlaw Hess and any administating users. 

To use this application you must first connect it with the bank database by pressing the "Connection" button above Pages.

Once connected you are then able to access data through the pages page.

Within this page you can get access to the accounts, overnight accured intrest of the accounts, Customers where you can view and create new customers,
Create and view ISA accounts allowing to delete and transfer accounts from one ISA to another and also view Transactions coming in and out of the bank.
